Metadata:
The data were derived from aerial LiDAR acquisition (UAV LiDAR) and photogrammetric processing of the Faculty of Engineering, Universitas Gadjah Mada. The height of each building was extracted from a normalized Digital Surface Model (nDSM) or Object Height Model (OHM). Building outlines were manually digitized from orthophotos.
